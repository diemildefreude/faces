memory sculpture by stephan e perez









The first faces added to this are of friends, family, colleagues from various times in my life.

The next could be of you or anyone else reading this.

Unlike a human's memory, you can choose whether to become part of it by touching become a face at the top of the page and recording your face and voice. Also unalike, you can simply observe by skipping to view faces.

Like a human memory, it will continue to grow and diverge. Even people whom I've never met might record their face. When I die, it can persist and grow further... provided someone pays the server and domain costs.
